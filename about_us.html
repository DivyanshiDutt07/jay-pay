About Us

Welcome to Jay Pay IT Solution Private Limited, incorporated on June 14, 2021.

We are proud to announce that we were recognized as the Innovative Start-up of the Year at the Icons of Asia 2023. This prestigious accolade reflects our dedication to delivering cutting-edge solutions and our impact in the financial technology landscape.

Under our brand, Folow Future, we specialize in innovative software solutions designed to streamline petty cash management. Our commitment to enhancing financial efficiency has made us a trusted partner for businesses seeking to optimize their cash flow processes.

At Jay Pay, we pride ourselves on our robust collaboration with over 52 banks and non-banking financial companies (NBFCs), enabling us to offer a comprehensive range of loan products and financial services. Our expertise and dedication to client success drive us to continuously improve our offerings, ensuring that we meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Join us on a journey towards smarter financial management as we shape the future of petty cash solutions with many more upcoming projects.
